Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma)

4-year undergraduate program covering pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, and clinical pharmacy.

  • Eligibility: 12th with PCM/PCB
  • Seats: 100
  • Affiliation: University of Magadh

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma)

2-year diploma program focusing on pharmaceutical practice and community pharmacy.
