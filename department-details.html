Our Departments

Specialized departments with expert faculty and modern facilities

Department of Pharmaceutics

Focuses on the design and development of drug delivery systems, formulation sciences, and pharmaceutical technology.

Key Areas:
  • Drug Formulation & Development
  • Novel Drug Delivery Systems
  • Pharmaceutical Technology
  • Biopharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics
Faculty: 8 Members

Department of Pharmacology

Studies the effects of drugs on biological systems and the mechanisms of drug action in the human body.

Key Areas:
  • Clinical Pharmacology
  • Toxicology & Drug Safety
  • Pharmacovigilance
  • Therapeutic Drug Monitoring
Faculty: 6 Members

Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Involves the study of drug design, synthesis, and analysis of pharmaceutical compounds.

Key Areas:
  • Medicinal Chemistry
  • Drug Design & Synthesis
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Quality Control & Assurance
Faculty: 7 Members

Department of Pharmacognosy

Studies natural products and their medicinal properties, including plant-based pharmaceuticals.

Key Areas:
  • Natural Product Chemistry
  • Herbal Drug Development
  • Phytochemistry
  • Traditional Medicine Research
Faculty: 5 Members

Department Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities supporting research and education

Research Laboratories

Advanced research labs with modern equipment for pharmaceutical research.

Analytical Instruments

HPLC, GC-MS, UV-Vis spectrophotometers for drug analysis.

Medicinal Plant Garden

Botanical garden with medicinal plants for pharmacognosy studies.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with pharmaceutical software and databases.
