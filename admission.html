Admission Process for B.Pharma and D.Pharma

Follow these detailed steps to secure your admission

1
Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the minimum educational qualifications (usually 10+2 with Science subjects for B.Pharma and 10th or 10+2 for D.Pharma).

2
Application Form

Fill out the application form for the desired college or through the centralized/state-level admission portal.

3
Entrance Exam

Appear for relevant entrance exams such as state CET, GPAT, or institute-specific tests (some colleges offer direct admission based on merit).

4
Merit List & Result

Check the merit list or entrance exam results published by the authorities or colleges.

5
Counseling/Interview

Participate in counseling sessions or interviews (if required) for seat allotment and document verification.

6
Document Verification

Submit required documents such as mark sheets, certificates, ID proof, and photographs for verification.

7
Seat Allotment

Receive confirmation of seat allotment based on merit/rank and preferences.

8
Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat in the allotted college.

9
Reporting to College

Report to the college/institute on the specified date to begin the academic session.

Eligibility Criteria

B.Pharma Eligibility
  • Passed 12th standard from a recognized board
  • Must have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology
  • Minimum 50% marks in 12th (45% for reserved categories)
  • Valid NEET score or State CET score
  • Age limit: 17-25 years
  • Indian nationality required
D.Pharma Eligibility
  • Passed 12th standard from a recognized board
  • Must have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology
  • Minimum 45% marks in 12th (40% for reserved categories)
  • State CET score or merit-based selection
  • Age limit: 17-23 years
  • Indian nationality required

Requirements and Deadlines

Please ensure you have all the following documents ready for application

Required Documents
  • Recent passport size photograph
  • Aadhaar card (photocopy)
  • Permanent and correspondence address proof
  • Entrance test result (if applicable)
  • 12th standard mark sheet/certificate
  • Migration certificate (if required)
  • Transfer certificate (TC) from previous institution
  • Character certificate from last attended school/college
Additional Documents
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate (if required)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Any other document as specified by the institute
Important Note

All documents must be original with photocopies for verification. Ensure all certificates are attested by the issuing authority.
