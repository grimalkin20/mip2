Academic Excellence

Comprehensive pharmaceutical education programs designed to meet industry standards

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma)

A comprehensive 4-year undergraduate program that provides in-depth knowledge of pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, and healthcare management.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 12th with PCM/PCB
  • Practical Training Included
  • Industry Exposure
Learn More

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma)

A focused 2-year diploma program that provides essential pharmaceutical knowledge and practical skills for entry-level pharmacy positions.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 12th with PCM/PCB
  • Hands-on Training
  • Quick Career Entry
Learn More
