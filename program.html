Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma)

A comprehensive 4-year undergraduate program designed to provide students with extensive knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, and healthcare management.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Eligibility

12th with PCM/PCB (50% minimum)

Degree

Bachelor of Pharmacy

B.Pharma Curriculum

Comprehensive curriculum covering all aspects of pharmaceutical sciences

Year 1 & 2 - Foundation
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Biochemistry & Clinical Pathology
  • Computer Applications in Pharmacy
Year 3 & 4 - Specialization
  • Pharmacology & Toxicology
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Pharmaceutical Technology
  • Biopharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics
  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence
  • Clinical Pharmacy & Therapeutics

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma)

A focused 2-year diploma program that provides essential pharmaceutical knowledge and practical skills for entry-level pharmacy positions.

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility

12th with PCM/PCB (45% minimum)

Diploma

Diploma in Pharmacy

D.Pharma Curriculum

Practical-oriented curriculum for quick career entry

Year 1 - Core Subjects
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry I
  • Pharmaceutics I
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Biochemistry & Clinical Pathology
  • Health Education & Community Pharmacy
Year 2 - Advanced Topics
  • Pharmacology & Toxicology
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry II
  • Pharmaceutics II
  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence
  • Drug Store & Business Management
  • Hospital & Clinical Pharmacy

Career Opportunities

Diverse career paths in the pharmaceutical industry

Community Pharmacist

Work in retail pharmacies, providing medication counseling and healthcare advice to patients.

Hospital Pharmacist

Manage medication therapy in hospitals and healthcare institutions.

Pharmaceutical Industry

Work in drug manufacturing, quality control, research and development.

Research & Development

Contribute to drug discovery and development in pharmaceutical companies.

Regulatory Affairs

Ensure compliance with pharmaceutical regulations and drug approval processes.

Academia

Pursue higher education and teaching careers in pharmaceutical sciences.
