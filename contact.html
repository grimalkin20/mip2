Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Candidates must have completed 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology with a minimum of 50% marks. They must also qualify in the entrance examination conducted by the institute or state-level entrance tests.

The fee structure varies by program. For B.Pharma, the annual fee is approximately ₹80,000, and for D.Pharma, it's ₹60,000. This includes tuition, laboratory, and library fees. Additional charges may apply for hostel and other facilities.

Yes, we offer various scholarships based on merit, need, and category. Government scholarships are also available for eligible students. Merit scholarships can cover up to 50% of the tuition fee for top performers.

Our placement cell maintains strong relationships with leading pharmaceutical companies. We have achieved a 95% placement rate with companies like Sun Pharma, Cipla, Dr. Reddy's, and many others. The average starting salary ranges from ₹3-6 lakhs per annum.

Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, mess facilities, recreation rooms, and 24/7 security. Hostel fees are separate from tuition fees.
