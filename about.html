Our Story

Established in 2017, Magadh Institute of Pharmacy has been at the forefront of pharmaceutical education in Bihar, India. Our institute was founded with a vision to create competent pharmacy professionals who can contribute to the healthcare sector and pharmaceutical industry.

Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to a recognized center of excellence in pharmaceutical education. Our commitment to quality education, research, and innovation has made us one of the preferred choices for students aspiring to build a career in pharmacy.

We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also ethical values, leadership qualities, and social responsibility among our students. Our graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the modern pharmaceutical industry and healthcare sector.
